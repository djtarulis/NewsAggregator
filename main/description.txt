News aggregator that pulls articles from various sources (using APIs like NewsAPI or web scraping) based on topics or keywords 
the user is interested in. The aggregator will then perform sentiment analysis on the articles to categorize them as positive, 
negative, or neutral. You can even display this information in a dashboard.
